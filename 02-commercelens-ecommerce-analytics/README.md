# CommerceLens – E-Commerce Analytics Platform

## Project Type

E-Commerce Analytics / Data-Driven Product / Dashboard / B2B & B2C

## Overview

CommerceLens is an e-commerce-focused analytics product developed for B2B and B2C users.

The product was designed to cover the full process from extracting data from e-commerce platforms to transforming that data into meaningful analysis and presenting the results through dashboard-based product experiences.

The main goal was to help businesses understand their e-commerce performance, customer behavior, product trends, and operational insights through a centralized and user-friendly analytics platform.

## Problem

E-commerce businesses often work with fragmented data across different platforms, sales channels, product records, customer activities, and performance indicators.

Without a centralized analytics structure, it becomes difficult to understand what is happening across the business, identify performance changes, and make fast data-driven decisions.

The challenge was to create a product that could bring e-commerce data together, analyze it, and present it in a way that was useful for both B2B and B2C decision-makers.

## Solution

CommerceLens was designed as a data-to-dashboard product for e-commerce platforms.

The product flow covered data extraction, data processing, analysis, and dashboard presentation. Instead of leaving users with raw data, the platform aimed to transform e-commerce data into clear, structured, and actionable insights.

The dashboards were designed to help users track key business metrics, understand product and customer behavior, and support faster decision-making processes.
