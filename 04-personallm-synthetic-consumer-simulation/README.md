# PersonaLLM – Synthetic Consumer Simulation Platform

## Project Type

Multi-Agent AI / Synthetic Persona Simulation / Decision Support / Product Development

## Overview

PersonaLLM is a multi-agent AI-based platform designed to help companies simulate potential customer reactions before launching a product, service, pricing strategy, campaign, or brand decision.

The platform focuses on creating synthetic consumer personas and simulated customer groups based on the brand, industry, and evaluation topic provided by the user. Instead of relying only on surveys or traditional market research methods, PersonaLLM aims to support decision-making by generating AI-powered consumer perspectives before a business decision is released to the market.

The main idea of the product is to help companies test strategic decisions earlier, faster, and with more contextual insight.

## Problem

Companies often need to understand how customers might react before launching a new product, service, pricing model, campaign, or brand strategy.

Traditional customer research methods can be time-consuming, costly, and difficult to repeat frequently. In many cases, companies make important decisions with limited customer insight or after slow manual research processes.

The challenge was to create a product that could support early-stage decision-making by simulating different customer profiles and generating structured feedback based on realistic consumer behavior patterns.

## Solution

PersonaLLM was designed as a synthetic consumer simulation and autonomous decision support platform.

The system takes basic inputs such as brand name, industry, and evaluation topic. Based on this context, it creates relevant synthetic personas and consumer groups, then simulates how different customer types might react to the given business scenario.

The platform helps transform complex market evaluation questions into structured AI-supported insights that can support product, pricing, service, campaign, and brand strategy decisions.
