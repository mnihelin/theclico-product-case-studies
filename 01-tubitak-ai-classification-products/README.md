# TÜBİTAK-Supported AI Classification Products

## Project Type

AI / Text Classification / LLM Services / R&D Productization

## Overview

This case study summarizes my contribution to two separate TÜBİTAK-supported R&D projects developed at TheClico.

The projects focused on fine-tuned text classification models capable of extracting multiple analytical perspectives from text-based inputs. I was involved from the project writing and planning stage and actively contributed across all TÜBİTAK work packages throughout the project lifecycle.

As the projects progressed, the outputs evolved into TheClico’s LLM services, which supported text-based analysis, classification, and AI-powered product development use cases.

The final work package focused on transforming the R&D outputs into user-friendly product interfaces. This productization and interface development stage was one of my main responsibilities within the company.

## Problem

The projects had strong AI and R&D value, but the outputs needed to be structured, managed, and transformed into product-oriented deliverables across multiple work packages.

## Solution

The project work packages were coordinated around the goal of transforming AI classification capabilities into usable product experiences.

The final productization stage translated the AI classification workflows into user-facing product flows. Users could enter text-based inputs, trigger analysis processes, and review AI-generated classification results through structured and understandable screens.

This helped transform the models and TheClico LLM service outputs from technical R&D deliverables into more accessible, testable, and demonstrable product experiences.
