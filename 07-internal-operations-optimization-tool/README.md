# Internal Operations & CX PoC Acceleration Platform

## Project Type

Internal Tool / CX Analytics Service / PoC Acceleration / Operations Management / Product Development

## Overview

This case study summarizes my contribution to a company-only internal platform developed at TheClico.

The platform was designed for TheClico employees to access internal workflows, operational modules, and the company’s core CX analytics service from a centralized interface. Instead of using fragmented tools and manually managing different processes across separate platforms, employees could select the relevant module from a sidebar and continue through a structured workflow.

One of the most important parts of this platform was the integration of TheClico’s main CX service. This service supported the end-to-end process of collecting customer experience data from nearly 50 supported channels such as social media, complaint platforms, app stores, and e-commerce sources; connecting the collected data to MongoDB; displaying and managing the data through a custom interface; exporting it in Excel format; classifying and filtering the data; and preparing it for visual, textual, dashboard-based, or report-based analysis.

The internal platform helped the company use this CX service more efficiently during PoC processes and respond faster to customer needs.

## Problem

As a fast-moving startup, TheClico needed to stay agile, produce PoCs frequently, and respond quickly to market demands.

However, as the company grew, internal information and workflows started to become fragmented across tools such as Notion and Miro. This created several problems:

* Internal knowledge was scattered across different tools
* Past work and previous outputs were harder to access
* Operational workflows were not fully centralized
* PoC preparation required repeated manual effort
* CX-related data collection and analysis workflows needed to be easier to run internally
* Different teams needed a more structured way to access services, reports, tests, investments, events, and project-related information

The challenge was to create a company-only internal platform that could centralize these workflows and help employees move faster.

## Solution

A centralized internal platform was designed with a sidebar-based structure.

Employees could select the relevant module from the sidebar and continue through the related workflow. The platform included internal operational areas as well as access to TheClico’s core CX service.

Within the CX service flow, employees could collect data from supported channels, connect and store the data through MongoDB, view the collected data in a custom interface, make additions or removals, export outputs in Excel format, classify the data, apply filtered classification processes, and prepare the results for analysis.

The system also supported a report-oriented flow where the steps before the final dashboard stage could be completed and presented as a structured report.

In addition to the CX service flow, the internal platform supported other company needs such as LLM tests, analysis reports, investment tracking, service follow-ups, event tracking, and internal project coordination.

## Business Value

The platform helped TheClico employees access internal workflows and the company’s core CX service from a centralized system.

By making the CX service easier to run internally, the team could prepare PoCs faster, collect and manage data more efficiently, classify and analyze outputs more consistently, and generate structured reports when needed.

The sidebar-based internal structure also reduced scattered information across different tools and helped standardize workflows such as LLM testing, analysis reporting, investment tracking, service management, event tracking, and internal project coordination.

This increased operational speed helped the team run more sprints, respond faster to market needs, and prepare customer-specific PoCs more efficiently.

In summary, the platform turned TheClico’s internal operational needs and core CX service workflows into a centralized employee-only system that supported faster PoC development, better internal coordination, and new customer opportunities.
