# Customer Behavior Analytics Platform

## Project Type

CRM / Customer Analytics / Behavioral Data / Product Development / Decision Support

## Overview

This case study summarizes my contribution to a customer behavior analytics platform developed at TheClico.

The product was designed to help companies understand their customers through real behavioral data instead of relying only on surveys or self-reported feedback.

The platform focused on analyzing customer activity from CRM data, purchase behavior, and application usage patterns. The main goal was to help businesses understand what customers actually do, which products or services they interact with, and how their behavior changes over time.

## Problem

Companies often try to understand customers through surveys, interviews, or manually collected feedback. However, what customers say and what they actually do may not always be the same.

Traditional customer understanding methods can also be slow, incomplete, and difficult to keep updated.

The challenge was to create a product that could help businesses understand customers through real behavior, such as purchases, CRM records, and application usage data.

## Solution

The platform was designed to collect and analyze customer behavior signals from different sources such as CRM systems, purchase history, and application usage activity.

Instead of focusing only on survey-based customer insights, the product aimed to generate behavior-based analysis by tracking real customer actions.

The system helped transform customer activity data into structured insights that could support marketing, product, sales, and customer experience decisions.
